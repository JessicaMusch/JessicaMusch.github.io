Tiny Forest

Tiny Forest was an assignment I created in my 4th semester within a few weeks in Blender. My focus of the animation was the more cartoon-like look and therefore I wanted to create a simple and cute story that works great with this style.

Animation

Animation Process

The process behind Tiny Forest is simple: First I decided that I wanted to work with a simple toon-shader made out of colour ramps, outlines and grease pencil lines. Then I decided on the story which I wanted to work with the simple look of the shader. Then I modelled all my characters and the environment, where I payed special detail to the characters and used simple forms and sharp lines to help the toon-shader to stand out. After this I gave them bones and animated those to fit the story I wanted to portray. In the end I edited the rendered animation and exported it to Davinci Resolve Studio, where I added the sound and text animations.
