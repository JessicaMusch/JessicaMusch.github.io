Tiny Forest

Tiny Forest is a cute and short 3D animation about a stone who wants to show something to his friend, a mushroom, but first has to ask a forest spirit to give the mushroom legs so that he can walk.

Animation

Background of Tiny Forest

Tiny Forest was an assignment I created in my 4th semester within a few weeks in Blender. My focus of the animation was the more cartoon-like look and therefore I wanted to create a simple and cute story that works great with this style.

Animation Process

The process behind Tiny Forest is quite simple: First I decided that I wanted to work with a simple toon-shader made out of colour ramps, outlines and grease pencil lines. Then I decided on the story which I wanted to work with the simple look of the shader. Then I modelled all my characters and the environment, where I payed special detail to the characters and used simple forms and sharp lines to help the toon-shader to stand out. After this I gave them bones and animated those to fit the story I wanted to portray. In the end I edited the rendered animation in Davinci Resolve and added the sound and Text animations.
