Solo Project

Fall

Fall is a cosy and peaceful highscore hunting 2D game in which the player, as a forest witch, tries to collect the fruit that falls from the trees in autumn and avoids the pumpkins.

Gameplay

Background of Fall

I created Fall as an assignment in my 4th semester within a few weeks in Unity and worked on all aspects of the game myself - the game design, art and code. I kept the game design deliberately simple to make sure I had a completely finished and polished game by the given deadline. After the concept of the game was finished, I wrote the code and worked with placeholder objects till I finished the art in the end.

My Coding Process

As I worked on the game alone, I started with the fundamental functionalities of the game, like the movement of the character and the falling of the objects, and went from there more and more into the specifics. Those include the Points and Hearts given and lost by the falling items, the resulting Highscore, the increased spawnrate and falling speed over time, the UI and the sound effects.
