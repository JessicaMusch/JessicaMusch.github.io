Between Dice and Madness: A Classic Adventure

"Between Dice and Madness: A Classic Adventure" is a short film that chronicles the escapades of a Pen & Paper group and shows the group dynamics between stereotypical fantasy characters. It focuses on the 3D scenes that depict the "theatre of mind" of a regular Pen & Paper session and on the Motion Capture used for the 3D-characters.

This group project is done in the sixth-semester course “Motion Capture”. My role in the group includes the 3D-modelling of our self-made models for the 3D dungeon that the players enter, the playing of one of the characters both in the real-life footage and in the Motion Capture recordings, the making of the physical terrain that will serve as the centrepiece on the players table during the real-life shots, and painting the minifigures, which are also used in the real-life shots.
