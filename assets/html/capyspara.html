Capyspara

We created Capyspara as an assignment while I was in my 6th semester. The goal was to develop a complete game and start the publishing process by the end of the semester. We have decided to develop a mobile game and tried to publish it to the Play Store at the end of the semester.

Gallery

Development

The game design for Capyspara was developed in the group and everyone contributed to the final idea. For the development I was solely focused on programming the matching game and adding the different items into the game, while the others worked on either the art or the programming of the other features. In the end I have also helped out with creating the UI and adding it into the game.

Final Result

During the semester we have repeatedly encountered major bugs and problems with the game, which held us back adding new features. In the end we were unfortunately not able to fix all major bugs and include all the features we had planned. While we have sent in a working game to the Play Store to review, our game is currently waiting for playtesters and we have decided that we will not provide the necessary testers and publish the game until we have fixed all major bugs. Unfortunately after the assignment got handed in, our group went different ways and therefore, we were not able to publish the game to the Play Store.
